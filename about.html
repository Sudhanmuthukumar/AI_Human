About TextOrigin

TextOrigin is a lightweight AI vs. Human text detector built to help you understand the origin of any piece of writing. Whether you’re a student, educator, or professional, our tool provides quick and transparent insights into whether text is more likely written by a human or generated by AI.

Our Outstanding Features

  • Readability: We calculate the Flesch Reading Ease score, giving you a sense of how easy or difficult your text is to read.
  • Vocabulary Diversity: We measure the ratio of unique words to total words, helping you see how rich and varied the language is.
  • Average Sentence Length: We analyze sentence structure by computing the average number of words per sentence, a key indicator of clarity and style.

By combining these linguistic features with advanced machine learning models like XGBoost and Logistic Regression, TextOrigin delivers not just a prediction, but also meaningful context behind the result. Our goal is to make AI detection transparent, educational, and easy to use.
